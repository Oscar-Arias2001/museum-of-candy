Museum of Candy

Founded in 1925 as a small local exhibition celebrating traditional sweets. Over the decades, it grew into a vibrant destination where visitors can explore the history of confectionery, discover rare recipes, and experience interactive displays that bring the magic of candy-making to life. Today, it stands as a colorful tribute to the joy and creativity that candy has inspired across generations.

Interactive Experiences

At the Museum of Candy, visitors are invited to step beyond the exhibits and immerse themselves in interactive experiences. From the lively Candy Lab, where guests can experiment with creating their own sweet treats, to the Virtual Tasting stations that let you explore imaginative flavors from around the world, every corner is designed to spark curiosity and delight.

Photo gallery

One of the most captivating is the photo gallery, where visitors can admire a collection of vibrant images that capture the artistry and history of sweets across generations. From vintage snapshots of traditional candy-making to modern photographs celebrating colorful creations, this exhibit offers a visual journey that brings the story of candy to life in a unique and memorable way.
